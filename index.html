icey_ecosystems

Arctic sea ice light transmission for ecosystem dynamics

About

This project estimates under-ice photosynthetically active radiation (PAR) across the Arctic Ocean by coupling ICESat-2 sea ice thickness and snow depth with ERA5 cloud cover inside a Beer-Lambert light transmission model. Results are compared across three Arctic summers (2019–2021).
